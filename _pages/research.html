Human Localization and Tracking

    Human Localization and Activity Recognition (via WiFi, IMU, Camera, etc.), this topic involves detecting a person's location and behaviors for applications in health monitoring, smart home, etc.

Scene Understanding based on Computer Vision

    involves analyzing visual inputs (e.g., images or videos) to interpret and classify objects, environments, and spatial relationships within a scene. Applications include autonomous driving (identifying road conditions and obstacles), robotics (navigating and interacting with surroundings), augmented reality (overlaying virtual objects), and surveillance systems (detecting activities and potential threats). It enhances human-computer interaction and decision-making.

Autonomous Systems

    Autonomous Systems, including robots, vehicles, and drones, are self-operating machines that use sensors, AI, and algorithms to perceive, decide, and act without human intervention. Applications range from delivery and exploration to manufacturing, surveillance, and transportation.
