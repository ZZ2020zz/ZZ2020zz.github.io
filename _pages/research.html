Research Topics

Research Topic 1

Wireless energy harvesting and wireless power transfer technologies (via far-field RF, mmWave and near-field coupling)

  • • We have developed world-leading wireless energy harvesting systems to capture energy from ambient wireless signals with state-of-the-art efficiencies.
  • • We have showcased the first real-world sensors fully powered by the wireless energy harvester.
  • • We are pioneering the research in wireless power transfer via mmWave signals.
