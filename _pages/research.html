Research Topics

Research Topic 1

Wireless energy harvesting and wireless power transfer technologies (via far-field RF, mmWave and near-field coupling)

  • We have developed world-leading wireless energy harvesting systems to capture energy from ambient wireless signals with state-of-the-art efficiencies.
  • We have showcased the first real-world sensors fully powered by the wireless energy harvester.
  • We are pioneering the research in wireless power transfer via mmWave signals.

Research Topic 2

Stretchable and flexible electronics, sensors and RF devices, mechanically reconfigurable antennas and on-body flexible devices

  • We are developing emerging flexible antennas and circuits using advanced soft materials.
  • We aim to enable state-of-the-art on-body wireless connectivity, remote sensing and power autonomy for human-to-machine intelligence and smart healthcare.
  • This research is conducted in collaboration with researchers from material science, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering.
